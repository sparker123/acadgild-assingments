Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a reciepe for deliciously rich scrambelled eggs.

Ingredeiants

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once the butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centere for every 20seconds(as if you are making an omlette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate untilserved)
